<b>Tormented Player</b> is the unofficial mobile version of <a href="https://www.tormentedradio.com/">Tormented Radio</a> by <a href="https://twitter.com/djdead">@djdead</a>.

<h2>Features:</h2>
• Ads free;
• Album name and cover art provided by <a href="https://www.last.fm/home">Last.fm</a>
• Play/stop from the Notification bar;

<h3>This app is open source!</h3>
Check out the source code and feel free to contribute on GitHub:
