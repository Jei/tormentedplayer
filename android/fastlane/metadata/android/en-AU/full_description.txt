<b>Tormented Player</b> is the unofficial mobile version of <a href="https://www.tormentedradio.com/">Tormented Radio</a> by <a href="https://twitter.com/djdead">@djdead</a>.
Listen to your favourite industrial, EBM and synthpop music on your Android device.

<h2><b>Features:</b></h2>
• Ads free;
• See a list of recently played songs;
• Album name and cover art provided by <a href="https://www.last.fm/home">Last.fm</a>;
• Play/stop the radio from the media notification;

For feedbacks and bug reports please send and email to support@tormentedradio.com.
